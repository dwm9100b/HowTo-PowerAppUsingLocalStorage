1. Go to PowerApps
  2. First thing to do, change to a dev environment, upper right under Environment
  3. Then in the left nav, click on Apps
  4. Top toolbar, click on +New App
  5. Click on Canvas
  6. This will create a Canvas app from blank
  7. Give it a name: My Tutorial App
  8. Format: Tablet
  9. Press Create
  10. Once created, click on the Settings cog in the toolbar above the formula text box
  11. In the Settings popup, click on Upcoming features
  12. Scroll down until you get to:
    1. Enable SaveData, LoadData, ClearData on web player
    2. Turn this on
  13. Close the popup
  14. Go ahead and rename the default Screen1 to: Main Screen
  15. Before anything else, let's make sure to do an initial save
  16. Upper toolbar icons towards the right, click on the Save icon
  17. Once its saved, we are ready to move on
